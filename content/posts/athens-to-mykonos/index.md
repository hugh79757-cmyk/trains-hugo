## [Athens](https://flights.techpawz.com/posts/cheapest-flights-chicago-to-athens/) to [Mykonos](https://cruise.techpawz.com/posts/mykonos_shore-excursions/): Your Options at a Glance

Traveling from Athens to Mykonos offers various options, though the ferry stands out as the primary mode of transport. This route is well-traveled, connecting the busy capital of [Greece](https://visafree.techpawz.com/posts/greece-visa-free/) with one of its most famous islands.

## By Ferry

![athens-to-mykonos](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/athens-to-mykonos/body_2.jpg)


The ferry journey from Athens to Mykonos is a popular choice among travelers. The cost for a ferry ticket is approximately $18. The journey typically takes around 140 minutes, making it a relatively quick and convenient way to reach the island. Ferries frequently depart from the port of Piraeus, which is easily accessible from central Athens.

## Price and Time Comparison

![athens-to-mykonos](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/athens-to-mykonos/body_3.jpg)


When considering the price and time involved, the ferry is not only economical but also efficient. With a fare of $18 and a travel time of just 140 minutes, it offers a balance of cost and convenience that is hard to beat. Other modes of transportation, such as flights or buses, may not provide such a direct or timely service.

## Best Time to Book for Cheapest Fares

![athens-to-mykonos](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/athens-to-mykonos/body_4.jpg)


To secure the best fares for your ferry ride from Athens to Mykonos, it is advisable to book your tickets in advance. Peak tourist seasons can lead to higher prices and limited availability. Planning your trip during the shoulder seasons or booking several weeks ahead can help you find more favorable rates.

## Practical Tips for This Route

![athens-to-mykonos](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/athens-to-mykonos/body_5.jpg)


When preparing for your ferry journey, consider arriving at the port early to avoid any last-minute rush. Ensure you have your tickets ready and check the ferry schedule ahead of time, as departure times can vary. Additionally, be mindful of the weather, as it can affect sailing conditions. If you’re traveling during peak seasons, it may be wise to reserve your spot in advance to ensure a smooth trip.
