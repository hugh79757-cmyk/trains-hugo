## [Sarandë](https://ferry.techpawz.com/posts/sarand%C3%AB-to-corfu-ferry/) to [Corfu](https://ferry.techpawz.com/posts/corfu-to-kefalonia-ferry/): Your Options at a Glance

Traveling from Sarandë to Corfu offers a scenic and efficient route primarily through ferry services. The journey allows travelers to experience the stunning views of the Ionian Sea while enjoying a smooth transition between the two destinations.

## By Ferry

![sarand%C3%AB-to-corfu](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/sarand%C3%AB-to-corfu/body_2.jpg)


The most convenient and popular method to travel from Sarandë to Corfu is by ferry. The ferry ride takes approximately 30 minutes, providing a quick and direct connection between the two locations. The cost for this ferry trip is $12, which is a reasonable fare considering the beautiful maritime experience it offers. Ferries operate regularly, making it easy to find a suitable departure time that fits your schedule.

When considering transportation options, the ferry stands out due to its short travel time of just 30 minutes. ## Best Time to Book for Cheapest Fares

![sarand%C3%AB-to-corfu](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/sarand%C3%AB-to-corfu/body_4.jpg)


Booking your ferry tickets in advance is advisable to secure the best fares. It is recommended to check for availability and make reservations as soon as your travel plans are confirmed.

## Practical Tips for This Route

![sarand%C3%AB-to-corfu](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/sarand%C3%AB-to-corfu/body_5.jpg)


When planning your ferry trip from Sarandë to Corfu, consider arriving at the ferry terminal at least 30 minutes before departure. This allows ample time for ticketing and boarding. Keep an eye on the weather, as rough seas can occasionally lead to delays or cancellations. Additionally, ensure you have your travel documents ready, as you will be crossing an international border. Lastly, while the ferry ride is short, it is advisable to bring along snacks and water, especially during the summer months when the sun can be quite strong.

In summary, the ferry from Sarandë to Corfu is the best option for travelers seeking a quick and enjoyable journey. With its reasonable fare and scenic views, it provides an efficient means of transportation between these two beautiful locations.
